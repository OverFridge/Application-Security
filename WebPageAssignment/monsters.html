Monster Information

There are a variety of large monsters in the Monster Hunter Series. They are divided into six types.

    The six types are
  • Brute Wyverns
  • Bird Wyverns
  • Flying Wyverns
  • Piscine Wyverns
  • Fanged Wyverns
  • Elder Dragons
